Buttermilk pancakes

1 c. flour
1 T. sugar
1 t. baking powder
1/4 t. baking soda
1/4 t. salt
1 egg
1 c. buttermilk
1 T. oil
milk 

Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl.  In another smaller bowl beat egg then add buttermilk and oil.  Pour
wet ingredients into dry ingredients and add milk to reach desired loose consistency.  Do not overmix, the batter
should be lumpy.  Pour 1/2 c. onto hot buttered griddle.  Flip when bubbly and brown.
